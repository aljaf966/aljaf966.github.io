American Psycho

Film directed by Marry Harron (2000), based off of the novel written by Bret Easton Ellis

Venmo

Patrick Bateman, the protaginst, is obsessed with the idea of wealth and power as he works as an investment banker in a law firm on Wall Street. He also spends a ridiculous amount of money on keeping up with his high matience appearance with. Telling the story via this platform can encapsulate in brief statements how much the story revolves around money. Venmo is a mobile app that allows for quick money transactions and to make comments regarding the transactions.

IMessege

As the story contains frequent conversations, and revolves around group settings/dialouge, telling the story via Imessege allows for quick back & forth communication as well as seperation in detail conversations between different characters.

Instagram

Bateman is a classic narcissist who is obsessed with his looks to the outside, Instagram is a platform that breeds and feeds off of narcissism as it allows people to display and flaunt every aspect of their lives. The story told on instagram allows it ot be shown from only Bateman's POV and what he chooses to filter and show the world.
